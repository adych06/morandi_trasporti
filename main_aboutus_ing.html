ABOUT US

Morandi Trasporti is the ideal partner for your trips. Specialized in bus rental and city tours, we offer a modern fleet and personalized services for every travel need. From bus rental for events and school trips to guided tours of the most beautiful cities of art, we guarantee comfort, safety and professionalism. Our experienced drivers and qualified guides will accompany you on an unforgettable travel experience.

Our Story

Since 1924, Morandi Trasporti has represented tradition and innovation in the world of transport. A century of experience has made us leaders in the industry, combining the passion of three generations with a cutting-edge fleet. Specialized in bus rental and city tours, we offer high quality services for every need: from school trips to corporate events, cultural tours to group transfers. Our century-old history is a guarantee of reliability, safety and professionalism.
